AI-Based Mental Health Screening Informed Consent

MindSpace may analyse typed responses, voice recordings, and facial expression metrics to identify patterns associated with mental health conditions such as stress, anxiety, depression, and mood changes. This system is for screening support only.

warning

Important Notice

Please understand this before continuing.

MindSpace does not provide a medical diagnosis or treatment recommendation. It only identifies acoustic, linguistic, and visual patterns. Any clinical action must be reviewed by a qualified mental health professional.

How Your Data May Be Used

Your data is used for screening, model validation, and research improvement.

Allowed use
  • AI model training and validation
  • Mental health screening research
  • Aggregated academic publication
Not allowed
  • No commercial advertising use
  • No sale of personal health data
  • No clinical diagnosis by AI alone
Security

Personal identifiers are separated where possible and data should be stored with encryption and access controls.

Retention

Raw recordings should be deleted after processing according to your system policy. Anonymised features may be retained for research.

view_module

Consent Modules

You can allow one, two, or all three data-processing modules.

Text Data

Typed responses, questionnaire answers, and written reflections may be analysed for linguistic patterns and emotional tone.

Voice Data

Audio recordings may be analysed for pitch, pace, rhythm, vocal energy, and acoustic features. This is not voice recognition.

Facial Expression Metrics

Camera-based facial metrics may be analysed for expression dynamics. This is not facial recognition or identity tracking.

Optional Research Sharing

Fully anonymised data may be shared for academic research only when you separately allow it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MindSpace a medical service?

MindSpace is a digital well-being support platform. It is not a replacement for emergency care or professional medical treatment.

Can I reset my password?

Yes. Use the forgot password page and enter your registered email address to start the recovery process.

Is my data private?

Your privacy matters. MindSpace should be designed to protect user data and avoid unnecessary sharing of personal information.

How do I contact support?

You can use the contact page to send a message. The team can reply based on the contact details you provide.

Important: If someone is in immediate danger or experiencing a crisis, contact local emergency services immediately. MindSpace is not an emergency response service.
